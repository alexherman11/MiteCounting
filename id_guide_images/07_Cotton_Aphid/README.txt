Cotton Aphid (Aphis gossypii)
==============================
Category: Pest

Images:
  cotton_aphid_1.png - Cotton aphid reference
  cotton_aphid_2.jpeg - Cotton aphid reference

ID Notes:
  - Highly variable in body size and color
  - Adults may be winged or wingless
  - Nymphs and adults of wingless cotton aphids vary in color from yellow to green to nearly black
  - Winged form have a dusty wax coating and are typically greenish blue, or amber and blue

Source: UC IPM - https://ipm.ucanr.edu/agriculture/cotton/cotton-aphid/
