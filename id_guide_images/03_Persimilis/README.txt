Persimilis (Phytoseiulus persimilis)
=====================================
Category: Predator

Images:
  persimilis_with_tssm_overview.png - Overview image showing Persimilis with TSSM
  persimilis_reference.png - Persimilis reference image
  persimilis_egg_vs_tssm_egg.jpeg - Persimilis egg (left) next to TSSM egg (right)

ID Notes:
  - Specific predator on two-spotted spider mite
  - Adults are pear/teardrop shaped, clear to reddish orange
  - Slightly larger than TSSM, have longer legs, and move faster
  - May be shinier than TSSM and less hairy
  - Nymphs are like smaller adults, may be lighter color
  - Eggs are oval, clear to somewhat orange and approximately twice as large as TSSM eggs

Source: UC IPM - https://ipm.ucanr.edu/natural-enemies/phytoseiulus-predatory-mites/
