Lewis Spider Mite (Eotetranychus lewisi)
==========================================
Category: Pest

Images:
  lewis_vs_tssm_adults.jpg - Upper two mites: adult Lewis; Lower two mites: TSSM
  lewis_vs_tssm_nymphs.jpg - Upper: Lewis mite nymph; Lower: TSSM nymph
  lewis_mite_nymph.jpg - Lewis mite nymph
  lewis_mite_reference.png - Lewis mite reference image

ID Notes:
  - Adults are smaller than TSSM
  - They have black speckles rather than two clear large dots
  - Narrower, may be lighter green than TSSM
  - (Never is red?)
